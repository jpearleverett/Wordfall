# Wordfall Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026

## Data We Collect

### Gameplay Data
- Puzzle completion statistics, scores, progress
- Game mode preferences and play patterns
- In-app purchase history

### Device Information
- Device type and OS version
- Anonymized device identifier for analytics
- Timezone for daily challenge synchronization

### Account Data (Optional)
- Anonymous authentication token (Firebase Anonymous Auth)
- Display name and club membership (if provided)

## How We Use Your Data
- Save progress and sync across devices
- Improve game balance and fix bugs
- Process in-app purchases
- Enable social features (clubs, leaderboards)

## Data Storage
- Local data via AsyncStorage (on-device)
- Cloud data in Google Firebase with encryption in transit
- Data retained for account lifetime; deleted on request

## Third-Party Services
- Firebase (Google): Auth, storage, analytics
- Apple App Store / Google Play: Purchase processing

## Children's Privacy
Rated 4+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parental spending controls available in Settings.

## Your Rights
- Access, delete, or export your data
- Disable analytics in Settings
- Contact: privacy@wordfall.game

## In-App Purchases
- Processed through App Store / Play Store
- Optional spending controls (monthly limits, PIN)
- No loot boxes; all probabilities displayed
