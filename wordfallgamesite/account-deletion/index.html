Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Wordfall account and all associated data at any time. This page exists so you can request deletion without needing to install or reopen the app.

Heads up: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any unspent gems, purchased VIP time, tournament progress, and club data will be lost.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (username, avatar, stats, prestige, chapter progress)
  • Your cloud save (gems, coins, inventory, completion history)
  • Club membership, contributions, and chat messages you authored
  • Friends list, blocked users, and notification tokens
  • Consent records and preference settings
  • Your Firebase Authentication record (sign-in identity)

What is retained (and why)

  • Purchase receipts — retained in anonymized form (your user ID is replaced with a one-way SHA-256 hash) for up to 7 years to meet tax and fraud-prevention obligations under applicable law. These records cannot be linked back to you.
  • Aggregated analytics — non-identifying usage metrics (e.g., "n daily active users") that do not contain any personal identifiers.

How to delete from inside the app

If you still have Wordfall installed, the fastest way is: open the app → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account & Data. Deletion is immediate.

Request deletion by email

Fill in the form below. We will verify the request, delete your data within 30 days (usually much sooner), and email a confirmation.

What happens next

  • We receive your request at info@iridescent-games.com.
  • We verify ownership (we may reply asking for proof, e.g. a Play Store order ID).
  • Deletion is executed on our servers within 30 days of verification.
  • You receive an email confirmation when it is complete.

Questions? Email info@iridescent-games.com or visit our Support page.
